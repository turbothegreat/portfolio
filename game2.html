Zombie Survivors Co-op

A fast-paced multiplayer survival game built in Unity where two players must work together to collect maps and fend off zombie waves!

About the Game

This is a 3D co-op shooter made with Unity. One player collects critical intel (maps) while the other defends with firearms against incoming waves. Teamwork is essential to win before the timer runs out!
