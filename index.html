Hi,

I’m Tiisang Mokgwabone, a passionate and professional Game Developer and Designer with nearly five years of experience. I specialize in crafting immersive games—2D, 3D, and board games—and I often work independently to bring unique ideas to life.

As the creative director of my own projects, I take pride in building exciting game mechanics, visual aesthetics, and rich environments, while also collaborating with talented people in other departments.
